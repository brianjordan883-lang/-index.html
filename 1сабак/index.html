Freen Sarocha Chankimha

Sarocha Chankimha (Thai: สโรชา จันทร์กิมฮะ; born 8 August 1998), nicknamed Freen (Thai: ฟรีน), is a Thai actress, super model, and singer.[1] Freen began her acting career in 2021 with the drama So Fit and later appeared in the television series Gap (2022). In 2024, she starred in the films Uranus 2324 and Rider. In January 2025, she was appointed a brand ambassador for the Italian fashion house Valentino.
